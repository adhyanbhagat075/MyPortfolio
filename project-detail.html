E-commerce Mobile App Redesign

A complete redesign focusing on improving the checkout flow and enhancing product discoverability, resulting in a **15% lift in mobile conversions**.

My Role

Lead UX/UI Designer

Timeline

4 Weeks

Tools Used

Figma, Maze, Illustrator

The Challenge

The legacy app had a cluttered navigation and a complex, multi-step checkout process, causing significant user drop-off.

The Process: Wireframing and Testing

I started with low-fidelity wireframes to rapidly test different approaches to the checkout form fields and information hierarchy. We focused on a single-page checkout structure to reduce friction.

The Solution

The final design features a **clean, modular UI** and a simplified three-step checkout process. The intuitive navigation structure allows users to jump between categories quickly.
